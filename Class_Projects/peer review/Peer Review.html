10 Most Common Web Design Mistakes

The article highlights the 10 common web design mistakes that can be avoided. Being qualified is not the only criteria for making impeccable web designs, being creative as well as being in a good mood is a necessary requirement. This is because, working as a web designer can be quite a challenging task, but if you are able to avoid these 10 most common mistakes when creating web pages, you will definitely excel in web contents creation.

Find Below The 10 Common Mistakes:

  1. Slow Loading Websites
  2. Video Over Text
  3. Non-Responsive Website
  4. No or Not Enough Header Tags
  5. No Contact Information
  6. Colour and Contrast
  7. Disorganization of Content
  8. Lack of Call-to-Action
  9. Weak SEO
  10. The Links Do Not Work
1. Slow Loading Websites

Avoid overloading your webpage with different pictures, animation and videos, because these will slow down your website. Thereby having negative impact on the bounce rate and your entire business. It is very important to note that simplicity and minimalism are key to a successful web design.

2. Video Over Text:

To be able to make people stay longer in your webpage, it is very important that your site must be very interactive as well as engaging so that people can access the information you intend to share.

3. Non-Responsive Website:

It is advisable that one should pay attention to how your webpage opens on mobile phones (not just computer), this is because almost everyone uses his or her mobile phone to access the internet.

4. No or Not Enough Header Tags:

Do not ignore the use of headings because they positively influence people´s perception thereby making users´ experience on your site positive and successful.

5. No Contact Information:

Feedback is very important in business. This is why it is a very essential to provide different sources of contacts so that visitors can keep in touch with you e.g. email phone numbers or even link to your social media accounts.

6. Colour and Contract:

It is always advisable to design visually attractive websites so that people will not leave it immediately they access it. It is very important to know how to work with colour combination.

7. Disorganization of Content:

There are a lot of techniques to use in designing your webpages to reduce the number of words as well as provide useful information e.g. Pie chart, tables, provide lists with bullet points.

8. Lack of Call – to – Action:

Every website has its own aim. It is of great importance to make sure that your website calls the users to action e.g. if it is an online shop, ensure that there are eye catching pictures, descriptions of beneficial features of your product as well as the “Buy Now “ button.

9. Weak SEO:

It is important to use different SEO tools if you want your website to be on the first page of search engines like google, bing, yahoo etc. example of SEO tool is Keywords. This can move your site higher as people type them and accessing your webpage thereby enhancing the numbers in traffic statistics.

10. The Links Do Not Work:

This is an avoidable mistake that is often made before launching the website due to lack of time. If the links do not work, visitors may not continue with the website because they may have formed the opinion that the website is a scam.
